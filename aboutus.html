About Us

At Project Identus, we specialize in matching unidentified remains with missing persons reports. Using advanced forensic techniques and collaboration with law enforcement, we work to bring closure to families by identifying the deceased and providing answers. Our mission is to help reunite families and offer support during difficult times.
